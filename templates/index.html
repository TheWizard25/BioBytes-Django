NEWSLETTER

A fortnightly newsletter

We will publish our newsletter 'BioBits' fortnightly. We will send it to students@iiitd.ac.in in digital format and will put up a printed version on notice boards. The content will largely pertain to the advancements in computational biology domain and important things happening in the CCB dept. of our college. We will attach resources for further reading and in some issues we might also include link to a problem (Rosalind's bioinformatics stronghold is a good resource). We shall consult with the professors and seniors for topics to include in every issue.
